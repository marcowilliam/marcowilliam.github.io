About

Marco William Paulo da Silva is 3rd-year Software Engineering Student who is currently enrolled at University of Brasília. He went to SCAD (Savannah College of Art and Design) in 2015 to study interactive design for a year. He speaks Portuguese, English, Spanish, and has a basic knowledge of French.

During his time studying at SCAD (a college of art and design), Marco has been diversifying his skills as a Software Engineer and developing a broader perspective on the world. On the interactive design side, Marco has knowledge in Illustrator, Photoshop, Maya, and After Effects. He combines these skills with programming experience with C, Java, PHP, Ruby on Rails, Grails, Python, Processing, Android, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Arduino.

From 2012 to 2014, Marco participated in a project where his function was to assure and to control the quality of applications for mobile devices of the Brazilian company Positivo Informática, gaining experience with Software Testing. Since he went back to Brazil (August, 2015) he is working for a project that is responsible for providing a hybrid reference software architecture for the Brazilian Ministry of Communications using Grails and Java.
